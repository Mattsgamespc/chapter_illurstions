The Alchemist

A fable about following your dream.

Paulo Coelho

It is the story of a shephard boy from the spainish province of Andalusia who dreams of travelling the world in search of a treasure as desairable as any ever found. From his home he journeys to exotic markets of North Africa and then into the Egyptian desert, where a fatefull encounter with the alchemist awaits him...

It is an inforgettable story about the essential wisdom of listening to our heart and, above all, following our dreams.
